Unnati

Established in 1998

We are a Non-Governmental Organization dedicated to providing basic education to economically disadvantaged children. We believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Our mission is to provide basic education to children from economically disadvantaged backgrounds, empowering them to make informed choices about their futures. By doing so, we aim to foster a sense of self-worth and potential in these children, thereby giving them the tools they need to break away from the constraints of poverty and lead fulfilling lives.
